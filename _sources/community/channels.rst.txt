Community channels
==================

So, where is the ValjangEngine community and where can you ask questions and get help?

Note that some of these channels are run and moderated by members of the ValjangEngine community or third parties.

A brief overview over these and other channels is also available on the `ValjangEngine website <https://Valjang.fr/community>`_.

Q&A
---

- `Official ValjangEngine Questions & Answers <https://Valjang.fr/qa/>`_

IRC on Freenode
---------------

- `General: #ValjangEngineengine <https://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=#ValjangEngineengine>`_
- `Engine development: #ValjangEngineengine-devel <https://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=#ValjangEngineengine-devel>`_
- `Documentation: #ValjangEngineengine-doc <https://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=#ValjangEngineengine-doc>`_
- `Pull request meetings: #ValjangEngineengine-meeting <https://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=#ValjangEngineengine-meeting>`_
- `GDNative: #ValjangEngineengine-gdnative <https://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=#ValjangEngineengine-gdnative>`_
- `Website and public relations: #ValjangEngineengine-atelier <https://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=#ValjangEngineengine-atelier>`_
- `IRC logs <https://ValjangEngine.eska.me/irc-logs/>`_
